# Code of Conduct: DSCI 522: Group15

# Introduction  
Our group is committed to building a collaborative, safe, and respectful environment for learning, sharing differing viewpoints & contributing. Everyone participating in this project whether through discussions, code contributions, documentation, or reviews is expected to help maintain a positive and supportive space.
This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions related to the project.

# Commitment to Diversity and Inclusion  
Our project welcomes individuals from all backgrounds, regardless of age, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, race, religion, education, or any other characteristic. We strive to ensure that every participant feels respected, valued and able to contribute without fear of discrimination or exclusion.

# Expected Behaviour  
All members of this project community are expected to act with professionalism, integrity, and openness. This includes:
- Using respectful and considerate language in all forms of communication
- Listening to others' viewpoints and recognizing their contributions
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback in a thoughtful manner
- Engaging in discussions that promote collaboration and mutual understanding

# Inappropriate Behavior  
- Harassment of any participant in any form
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Violent threats or language directed at another person
- Sharing or displaying offensive, explicit, or inappropriate material
- Using slurs, excessive profanity or aggressive or hostile language

All members are expected to refrain from all forms of unacceptable conduct to maintain a respectful environment.

# Consequences for Unacceptable Behavior  
If a participant engages in inappropriate behavior, they will be asked to stop and comply immediately. If the behavior continues or the violation is serious, the matter may be escalated to the teaching team or course faculty. Continued violations may result in removal from the project.

# Reporting Concerns and Guidelines  
If a participant violates this Code of Conduct, the incident may be reported by contacting Raghav Gupta at raghav90@student.ubc.ca. All reports will be treated seriously and handled with discretion. The Group 15 team will review the situation and take actions it considers appropriate given the circumstances.
